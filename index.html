About me

A zoologist and science communicator, working at the University of Oxford and the University of Reading. Behavioural ecologist and conservation biologist with an interest in invasive species.
Winner of FameLab Poland and I’m a Scientist, get me out of here! Presenter for the Discovery Channel. Bilingual: Polish and English. Find out more →
