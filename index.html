About me

I am a zoologist and science communicator, working at the University of Oxford and the University of Reading. I specialise in behavioural ecology and conservation biology.
Winner of FameLab Poland and I’m a Scientist, get me out of here! Presenter for the Discovery Channel. Bilingual: Polish and English. Find out more →
